How we help

Hands-on support, not handouts. Real advisory for real businesses.

Startup Support

For entrepreneurs in the first 24 months of operation. Covers business registration, bookkeeping setup, first financing applications, and the foundational planning work that puts a new business on solid legal and financial footing from day one.

Growth Advisory

For established small businesses ready to hire, expand, or access larger capital. Engagements include a structured growth plan, financial readiness assessment, and active support through the financing or grant application process.

Equity-Focused Programs

Dedicated programs for Indigenous entrepreneurs, newcomers, women-owned businesses, and youth entrepreneurs who face systemic barriers to capital and business development resources. All programs available in both official languages.

Who we serve

For entrepreneurs who have been underserved by the mainstream business support system.

Newcomers to Canada

Immigrants and refugees with business experience, professional skills, or a strong concept who need support navigating Canadian regulations, banking relationships, and government programs designed for businesses like theirs.

Indigenous entrepreneurs

First Nations, Métis, and Inuit business owners seeking culturally informed advisory support alongside access to Indigenous-specific financing programs including NACCA, regional Aboriginal Capital Corporations, and federal set-asides.

Main Street small businesses

Sole proprietors and small employers in trades, food service, retail, and personal services who need practical business help, not a consultant who has never run a business, and access to financing they may not know exists.
